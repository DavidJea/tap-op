Story

Tap-op is a little game I made with a lot of joy — it is my first app, and my first experiment with vibe coding. I built piece by piece, module by module, with curiosity and wonder, like playing the Lego which I never had as a kid. It is free and has no ads, no internet required, just tap to play. It's simple and intuitive, but what makes it special is how personal it can be: you can copy and paste images from your browser to create your own villains, record your own sound effects, and pick from quirky objects like eggs, cupcakes, or whatever makes you smile. Everything stays local on your device — unless you choose to export and share it yourself. Whether you're on a plane, on a break, or just need something lighthearted, Tap-op is your playful, customizable escape — and I hope it brings you as much joy as I had making it.
